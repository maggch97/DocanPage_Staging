Core Technology Highlights

Explore W1216's revolutionary technology for an unprecedented printing experience

Vision Auto-Locating

Automatically recognizes positioning and matches printing patterns for batch production with higher precision and efficiency.

  • Smart recognition eliminates need for molds
  • Precision printing on irregular shapes
  • Multi-pattern auto-matching without layout

Conveyor Printing with Cyclic Stepping

Features imported belts for continuous printing, paired with THK linear guides and servo motors for high-precision motion control and stable operation.

  • Imported belts ensure <0.05mm platform accuracy
  • Inovance dual servo motors guarantee high-precision operation
  • Japanese THK high-speed silent guides for superior stability

UV LED Curing System

Incorporates the latest UV LED curing technology for instant drying, eliminating wait times, significantly improving production efficiency, and reducing energy consumption for a more eco-friendly solution.

  • Dual-zone independent control for adjustable curing intensity based on different materials
  • Low-heat design suitable for heat-sensitive materials
  • 50,000-hour ultra-long lifespan, significantly reducing maintenance costs
